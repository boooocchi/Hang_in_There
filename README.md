<h2>🧐 Features</h2>

ーDashboard Pageー<br/>
A page where you can see the weather, wish list and your wardrobe capacity.
<img width='600' height='auto' src="https://github.com/boooocchi/Hang_in_There/assets/111376639/f5ae56d5-c688-436a-aabb-e6e0bd941823" alt="project-image">
<br/><br/>
ーWardrobe Pageー<br/>
Users can explore the wardrobe for their registered pieces of clothing.
<img width='600' height='auto' src="https://github.com/boooocchi/Hang_in_There/assets/111376639/389a5ad6-3e40-4775-8b15-f49eddf60e73" alt="project-image">
<br/><br/>
ーRegister Piece Pageー<br/>
This is where user can register their favorite piece of clothing.
<img width='600' height='auto' src="https://github.com/boooocchi/Hang_in_There/assets/111376639/ef3652e0-9eff-4cf6-9d25-94a889c2d4a1" alt="project-image">
<br/><br/>
ーDendo Outfit Pageー<br/>
Users can combine the items from the wardrobe to make an outfit and register it to this Dendo Outfit page.
<img width='600' height='auto' src="https://github.com/boooocchi/Hang_in_There/assets/111376639/21e5a4d0-d259-47bd-9073-773cb1d2619f" alt="project-image">
<br/><br/>
ーAI Suggestion Pageー<br/>
Users can select the item from the wardrobe and ask for matching pieces of clothing!!

<img width='600' height='auto' src="https://github.com/boooocchi/Hang_in_There/assets/111376639/5abca74a-b76f-4cdd-8dfc-d4e71714bc43" alt="project-image">
